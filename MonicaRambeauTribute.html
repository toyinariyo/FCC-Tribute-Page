Monica Rambeau

Here is the history of Monica Rambeau:

  • 1984 - Born to Maria and Frank Rambeau
  • 1990 - Frank Rambeau died*
  • 1995 - Monica and Maria Rambeau reunite with Carol Danvers
  • 1995 - Monica meets Skrulls.
  • 1995 - Monica says goodbye to Carol Danvers.
  • 2012 - Monica joins SWORD.
  • 2018 - Monica becomes a victim of the Blip while visiting her mother at the hospital.
  • October 2023 - Monica is brought back to life by Bruce Banner and finds out her mother died.
  • November 5th 2023 - Monica gets a mission to investigate the Westview Anomaly.
  • November 5th 2023 - Monica accidentally gets pulled into said anomaly.
  • November 7th 2023 - Monica is thrown out of the anomaly by Wanda Maximoff.
  • November 10th 2023 - Monica reenters the anomaly and gains superpowers.
  • November 10th 2023 - Monica uses her newfound powers to protect Billy and Tommy Maximoff from bullets.
  • November 10th 2023 - Monica gets a new mission from Nick Fury in space.

"I lost the person closest to me, too. The worst thing I can think of has already happened to me and I can't change it. I can't undo it. I can't control this pain anymore. And I don't think I want to, because it's my truth."

-- Monica Rambeau

* - It's unknown if Frank Rambeau died or simply disappeared in the MCU
